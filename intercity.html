Intercity

PoolCircle InterCity puts the flexibility and convenience of self-drive back into travel, while ensuring that the fuel cost doesn't put a hole in your pocket.

The joy of highway driving

Ease her into the 5th gear (it usually points North East on your gear shift) and cruise along with your favorite CD in the background. You wont find much traffic or hear too many honks here. So, the next time you are planning a trip, opt for the joy of stress free self-drive. Start early when the birds are still chirping

Who will you travel with today

Trains and buses don't let you choose who you ride with. PoolCircle does! Six hours on the road can seem like a very short time when you are having a great conversation. Our Trust Point based rating system gives you the information to make informed decisions abut who you are carpooling with.

Flexibility to travel, YOUR way

Trains and buses leave at specific times, not when YOU want them to - assuming the IRCTC loads you can find tickets at all. Want to stop for a coffee or a sandwich by the highway? Photography anyone? No problem! Check out your options at PoolCircle, even before you head to IRCTC or look for bus tickets online.

And its cheaper!!!

Sharing the fuel and toll cost with upto 3 other folks is actually cheaper than a 3 Tier AC ticket in the train or your AC bus ticket, and we are not even talking about the cost of commuting to and from the station (not to mention the haggling with autos and cabs to the station).

How does it work:

  • Choose InteCity option in Add Trip - Set up the travel details.
  • Contact available passengers / respond when they reach out.
  • Carpool together and the Car Owner gets paid the Fuel Share.
  • Rate the car owner - Help make PoolCircle safer.

Bangalore - Chennai

Yes, its slightly tricky until Hosur and after Poonamalle, but the 289 km of 6-lane highway in between offers an amazing driving experience. The hills around the Jolarpet-Yelagiri area are pretty breath taking sight along the way.

Kamat Upachar near Krishnagirl or the Saravana Bhavan at Vellore offer awesome South Indian fare on the way. Try this route once, and you will never want to take the train or bus for this route.

Mumbai - Pune

India's first six-lane concrete expressway, the Mumbai-Pune expressway offers an awesome one of the best driving experience in India. Winding through as many as 5 illuminated tunnels, this highway cuts down to travel time to less than a couple of hours.

Watch out for some scenic backdrops near the Khandala area, especially during monsooon. Yes, you should absolutely buy some awesome Chikkis on the way near Lonavla.

Delhi - Chandigarh

This is the route to take if you love to pop an occasional bite while on a road trip. The route passes through several small cities like Sonipat, Panipat and Karnal, and is approximately 250 Km. Occasional traffic slowdown maybe found near Karnal, but the rest is a smooth journey.

Havelis are found frequently, keep an eye for the 'Sukhdev Vaishno' dhaba in Sonipat where you get awesome parathas and lassi. Its right along the route, so no diversions!
