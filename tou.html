What is PoolCircle?

PoolCircle is a technology enabled social platform that enables users, called "Car Owners", who are driving their car or any other vehicle from point A to point B to offer rides to one or more users, called "Passengers", travelling in the same direction who are willing to ride along with the "Car Owner". The term "Car Owner" in the context of PoolCircle refers to the user who is offering seats in his or her vehicle and he or she may or may not be the actual Owner of the vehicle. The terms "Car Owner" and "Passenger" as also used purely in the scope of a single carpool transaction in PoolCircle.

The incentive for the Car Owner and Passenger to car pool or share rides might be to commute using a sustainable / eco-friendly manner or to save on fuel cost or to cut costs in comparison to more expensive commute option or to reduce traffic in the roads or to reduce driving stress.

PoolCircle provides capabilities for enterprises to offer this platform to their employees as a sustainable, safe and economical commute option.

PoolCircle does not own or operate cars or any vehicle for transporting its users. PoolCircle does not have any contract with its "Car Owners" or "Passengers" or provide guarantee / assurance that it's "Car Owners" will discover "Passengers" or vice versa.

How can it be used?

PoolCircle allows registered users to opt to be a "Car Owner" and offer rides during your trip by specifying the Starting Point, Destination and the Time of your commute. Alternately, the user may opt to be a "Passenger" and search for "Car Owners" in a specific route at a given time. PoolCircle will list the available "Car Owners" in the similar or nearby routes and the "Passenger" can "Request" a carpool with the "Car Owner".

The user can choose from an option of Schedules, OnDemand and Inter-city rides. They can also set up the trip as a one-way or a two-way trip. The user can choose the days of the week for the schedules.

When another user is found on the PoolCircle network going the same way, PoolCircle will list the available matches and the user can set up the carpool by either calling the person to coordinating the carpool. Alternately, PoolCircle also allows an option to send a message to the other person to coordinate the carpool. This message will be received by the other person as an SMS on their registered mobile number.

PoolCircle MyCircle feature allows you to define the set of users that you are willing to carpool with. You may choose one or more of "Office Colleagues" / LinkedIn Connections / Facebook Friends. You may choose to ride with Male Only / Female Only / Both Male and Female. PoolCircle will only show you users who are part of your MyCircle in all your searches. In addition, only users in your MyCircle will see your profile when they do a search. The MyCircle setting maybe modified for each carpool ride.

For your convenience, PoolCircle allows you to create Schedules for trips that you under take on a regular basis. Ex Office commutes every week day. PoolCircle will automatically publish trips on your behalf as per the Schedules that you have set up. Please ensure that you Delete all existing Schedules in case you want to stop using the product. This will ensure that trips are not published on your behalf and other users do not continue trying to carpool with you.

PoolCircle allow the user to invite his or her friends to join the network. They may choose to invite friends from Facebook / Contacts or Email in case of iOS and Android app. In case of web app, the user can invite Facebook friends and email contacts.PoolCircle allows user to rate others on the network as part of the safety feature. User can rate anyone on the platform and the other person can see the rating that you have provided.

Safety:

You are responsible for your safety w.r.t. the carpooling on PoolCircle. PoolCircle is only a technology enabled social platform helsp you to initiate, accept and undertake carpooling in order to use sustainable means of commuting, reduce fuel cost, reduce stress associated with driving and reduce overall traffic in the roads. You are encouraged to use PoolCircle responsibly and safely. PoolCircle recommends the following safety guidelines in the course of using our product.

  1. PoolCircle validates email addresses during registration using mechanism described in the "Who can user PoolCircle" section of this document. This pertains to the registrations done using the Email option. PoolCircle does not undertake any other user checks at an individual level.
  2. PoolCircle validates Google+ and Facebook based registrations using Google+ and Facebook based authentication respectively. This pertains to the registrations done using the Google+ or Facebook options.
  3. Please set up your My Circle definition carefully and verify the information provided while requesting a carpool with a "Car Owner". Follow similar caution while accepting a carpool request from a "Passenger".
  4. Carry a photo ID and show the same at the pickup point while take a ride and request the same from your ride partner.
  5. If you are a Passenger choose a safe pick up location which will not compromise your safety when you are waiting for the "Car Owner" to arrive.
  6. If you are a Car Owner choose a pick up location which does not compromise your safety on the road or disrupt traffic if you have to wait for the Passenger to arrive.
  7. As a Car Owner do not take in more Passengers than the recommended seating capacity of your vehicle
  8. Use of mobile phones while driving is extremely dangerous. Do not under any circumstances use your mobile phone either to coordinate a carpool using PoolCircle or for any other reason.
  9. PoolCircle recommends using the product to set up carpools before you start driving or to park your car in safe designated parking areas if you have to use the product or coordinate with your ride partner on the way.
  10. The Phone number of the other person with whom your ride match is done will be shown to you. Please use discretion in sharing it with others.
  11. Exercise overall caution and common sense while using PoolCircle
